How the Model Works
Methodology: Three weighted signals combine into win probability, simulated across 10,000 bracket runs each time you refresh.
📊
55% weight
Elo Rating
Modified FIFA Elo system. Head-to-head win probability:

P(A) = 1 / (1 + 10^((Elo_B - Elo_A) / 400))

A 400-point gap means ~91% win chance. Ratings run from ~1300 (Curacao) to ~2100 (Argentina).
⚽
30% weight
Tournament Form
Captures live performance, not just pre-tournament reputation:

• Points per game (0–9 scale)
• Goal difference (capped ±10)
• Goals scored / conceded

Removes recency blindness from Elo alone.
🏆
15% weight
Historical Pedigree
Accounts for tournament experience Elo cannot capture:

• Total World Cup appearances
• Number of titles won

Brazil (5 titles, 22 WCs) scores max. Normalised 0–1.
